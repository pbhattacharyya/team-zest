An app that prompts users to view everyday objects in a new way and meet people who share their perspectives and sense of humor.

Trigger

The urge to connect with like-minded people who appreciate their creative perspective and sense of humor is a strong trigger for millenials. A new challenge in the app everyday further motivates the user to keep coming back to the app.

Creative Act

The app encourages the user to view an everyday object for eg. a piece of bread from a different angle, draw it out and share it with people who have attempted the same challenge.

Variable Reward

As the system learns the user’s preferences for style and content of other people’s creations, the chance that it will show him/her something he/she enjoy increases. Outside the app, users find more delight in everyday objects.

Next Steps

Validate our assumptions: We assumed that people will be motivated to create to connect. But will they? We plan to talk to the target users to validate this assumption.

Explore other internal triggers: We liked how this idea has tapped into people’s emotional needs. We want to explore if we can apply some of these internal triggers in other ideas as well.
