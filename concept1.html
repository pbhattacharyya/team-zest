A smart mug with an e-ink screen connected to a mobile app where people can draw things and display on the mug. The idea is meant to help facilitate creative self-expression via drawing things.

Trigger

People like to express themselves novelly, so displaying digital drawings on a physical object is an excellent way for self-expression and a great trigger to start creating.

Creative Act

1) Users can easily select the module “I + verb” and changed it to “I love,” “I support,” etc. 2) Then they tap to enter the canvas and start drawing. 3) Users can choose a stencil and trace it if not good at drawing. 4) When finished, users can preview the drawing and publish it on the mug.

Variable Reward

The final drawing displays on the e-ink screen on the mug. As smart mug is a daily object, people can easily show their creation to others around them.

Aggregate Value

Since the mug is something people frequently use every day, it’s easier to get them back creating again and again than some pure mobile app. Also, as we plan to have a time limit on the display on the mug, this will also be a strong trigger to return once they see the screen is blank again.

Next Steps

Explore more objects Is mug the best place to display users’ creation? We will explore more physical objects to get inspirations.

Study e-ink screens We plan to study more of the e-ink screens, which will help us design and prototyping in the next two months.
