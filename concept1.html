A smart mug connected to a mobile app. Create designs on your phone and display them on the mug in real time.

Trigger

Events such as the Women’s March are strong external triggers that make people want to create things. Internally, millennials like to express themselves, which also gets them more motivated.

Creative Act

The creative act is writing and drawing. With all the templates and stencils we provide, our idea lowers the barrier to creation, so everyone can get started easily.

Variable Reward

The final drawing displays on the e-ink screen on a physical object, such as a mug. As smart mug is a daily object, people can easily show their creation to others around them.

Aggregate Value

People need time and effort to unlock the more complicated stencils we provide. This motivates them to keep coming back and start creating.

Next Steps

Explore more objects: Is mug the best place to display users’ creation? We will explore more physical objects to get inspirations.

Study e-ink screens: We plan to study more of the e-ink screens, which will help us design and prototyping in the next two months.

Test out stencils: As a key feature in the mobile part of the service, we will test out the stencils and provide most compatible and popular ones.
