An app that allows you to blend the physical and digital worlds to make creative, quirky artifacts.

Trigger

We believe that the desire to preserve a memory in a meaningful way is a strong trigger.

Creative Act

The creative act in the app is the ability to map your physical surroundings to a digital canvas real time with effortlessly.

Variable Reward

Users are able to create a unique, quirky and creative manifestation of their surroundings. The quality of the output depends upon the effort their put in.

Aggregate Value

Over time, users can accumulate a collections of textures from the real world. They also begin developing strong associative thinking skills that inform their creative practice.

Next Steps

Refine the internal trigger: Our first challenge will be to evaluate the strength of the internal trigger of saving a memory in this format is amongst millennials.

User test our prototype: We plan to talk to users to refine our creative act and make the interaction as simple and intuitive as possible.

Finding meaning in creation: What does fuzzy texture mean to you? Does that remind you of something? We plan to work towards finding a way to capture value and meaning in the act of creation that might be relevant for the user.
