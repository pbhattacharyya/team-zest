Our Framework

1. Provide a trigger.

Creativity is a loaded word. How might we make it more inclusive?

2. Lower the creative barrier.

The creative journey has highs and lows. How might we help more people along it?

3. Make the output satisfying.

Idle minutes are usually spent consuming mindless content. How might we turn these into creative moments?

4. Provide validation.

Our environment can jumpstart our creativity. How might we provide people with the triggers they need?
